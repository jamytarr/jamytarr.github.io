<h3 style="text-align: center;">When the weather cools off this fall, our Cape will come to the rescue. This incredibly soft light-weight cape made from the finest Japanese wool and Italian Lambskin will feel like your favorite blanket. Our Cape is elegantly tailored for a balanced fit in the shoulders. Classic details include a magnetic snap closure, lambskin neckline and arm vents, and a beautiful golden buckle finish this piece with style. </h3>
<h3 style="text-align: center;">This year, we are choosing to belt our Cape to accent the waist. Large statement belts are on trend for Fall 2017, but you can choose a delicate belt to offer a more conservative look. This versatile Cape can take you from day to night with only a quick change of your accessories.</h3>
<h3 style="text-align: center;">Our Cape will always add sophistication to your outfit whether you wear it over a dress out on the town, or over trousers, a sweater and your favorite oxfords to work. Keep stylish and warm with this fall essential.</h3>
<h3 style="text-align: center;">Stay tuned for new posts in the weeks to come on how to edge up this cape for that wild child in you. </h3>
