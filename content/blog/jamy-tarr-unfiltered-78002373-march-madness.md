<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/Hello_March_1024x1024-765587d1.jpg" alt="Hello March" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Award season is upon us, the holidays are behind us and winter is just about to come to an end -- March is here!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">While some time is still left until we can all enjoy the warmer temperatures, there are a number of exciting things to look forward to this March.</span></p>
<p><em>Cherry Blossoms</em></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Cherry Blossom" src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/Cherry_Blossom_large-765587d1.jpg" style="float: none;"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">There is nothing more spellbindingly beautiful than observing the natural world. Starting in March, Japan’s cherry trees, known as ‘</span><a href="http://en.rocketnews24.com/2014/03/20/know-your-sakura-japans-three-best-cherry-blossom-trees/" target="_blank" title="Cherry Blossom Sakura"><span style="font-weight: 400;">sakuras</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">’, begin to blossom. If you can’t make it to Tokyo, the city of </span><a href="http://www.tripadvisor.com/Tourism-g28970-Washington_DC_District_of_Columbia-Vacations.html" target="_blank" title="Washington D.C."><span style="font-weight: 400;">Washington DC</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> has beautiful cherry blooms just waiting for you to admire.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The </span><a href="http://www.nationalcherryblossomfestival.org/" target="_blank" title="Cherry Blossom Festival"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cherry Blossom Festival</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> in Washington DC ushers in the blooming season. Take a guided tour </span><a href="http://bikeandrolldc.com/tour/blossoms-by-bike/" target="_blank" title="Blossom Bike Tour "><span style="font-weight: 400;">by bike</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> or </span><a href="http://dc-cruises.com/" target="_blank" title="Washington DC Cruise"><span style="font-weight: 400;">by cruise</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to soak it all in and relax with some libations at the </span><a href="https://www.livingsocial.com/events/1574390-admission-to-cherry-blossom-beer-wine-festival?afsrc=1&amp;cvosrc=affiliate.cj.5658810&amp;utm_campaign=5658810&amp;utm_medium=affiliates&amp;utm_source=cj&amp;city_id=1-washington-d-c&amp;id=1574390-admission-to-cherry-blossom-beer-wine-festival&amp;msdc_id=229-fairfax-county&amp;ref=XUA0000COMJ000000000000ENUS0000000000000021052012&amp;utm_content=3724478" target="_blank" title="Cherry Blossom Beer + Wine Festival"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cherry Blossom Beer + Wine Festival</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<p><em>St. Patrick’s Day</em></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img alt="St. Patricks Day Parade Chicago" src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/St._Patricks_Day_River_large-765587d1.jpg" style="float: none;"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Every year on March 17, the Irish and the Irish-at-heart observe </span><a href="http://www.mercurynews.com/bay-area-living/ci_29580772/st-patricks-day-2016-bay-area-parades-beer" target="_blank" title="St. Patrick's Day Bay Area"><span style="font-weight: 400;">St. Patrick's Day</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. What began as a religious feast day for the patron saint of Ireland has become an international celebration that’s turned into many different forms.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">St. Patrick’s Day </span><a href="http://www.chicagostpatsparade.com/parade-and-river-dyeing.html"><span style="font-weight: 400;">parades</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, </span><a href="http://sf.funcheap.com/st-patricks-day-pub-crawl-sf/" target="_blank" title="Bay Area St. Patrick's Day Pub Crawl"><span style="font-weight: 400;">pub crawls</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, </span><a href="http://www.active.com/san-francisco-ca/running/distance-running-races/st-patrick-s-day-5k-2016" target="_blank" title="San Francisco St. Patrick's Day Running Marathon"><span style="font-weight: 400;">marathons</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> or a combination are hosted in your local area.</span></p>
<p><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">House of Cards Season 4 Debut</span></em></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/House_of_Cards_large-765587d1.jpg" alt="House of Cards Season 4" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Clear your schedule for an epic Netflix binge paired with a bottle of </span><a href="http://www.wine-searcher.com/find/union+co+underwood+pinot+noir+oregon+usa" target="_blank" title="Underwood Pinot Noir"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Underwood Pinot Noir</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and some (healthy?) snacks. This political drama left so many unanswered questions during the Season 3 finale. To pass the time until the release, reading reviews from top dogs like <a href="http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/house-cards-season-4-tv-872024" target="_blank" title="The Hollywood Reporter House of Cards Season 2">The </a></span><a href="#temp_created_link"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hollywood Reporter</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, </span><a href="http://www.ew.com/article/2016/03/02/house-of-cards-ew-review" target="_blank" title="Entertainment Weekly House of Cards Season 4"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Entertainment Weekly</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and </span><a href="http://variety.com/2016/tv/reviews/house-of-cards-season-4-review-netflix-kevin-spacey-robin-wright-michael-kelly-1201715232/" target="_blank" title="Variety House of Cards Season 4 review"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Variety</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> helps.</span></p>
<p><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">Vernal Equinox</span></em></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/Spring_Picnic_large-765587d1.jpg" alt="Spring picnic" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The </span><a href="http://earthsky.org/?p=68679" title="Vernal Equinox" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">vernal equinox</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> signals the beginning of spring in the Northern Hemisphere, marking that special moment when the sun crosses the celestial equator going from south to north. This year it’s happening on March 20.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Put away those </span><a href="http://www.refinery29.com/warm-stylish-sweaters" target="_blank" title="Winter sweaters"><span style="font-weight: 400;">sweaters</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, break out the colorful </span><a href="http://www.today.com/style/how-wear-versatile-handkerchief-dress-spring-laura-prepon-today-t77681" target="_blank" title="Dresses for spring weather"><span style="font-weight: 400;">dresses</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and </span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="http://jamytarr.com/collections/outerwear" title="J'Amy Tarr spring weight outerwear">spring-weight outerwear!</a></span></p>
<p><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">Whale Migration</span></em></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/Whale_Migration_large-765587d1.jpg" alt="Whale migration watching" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.7x7.com/play/best-coastal-perches-whale-watching-northern-california" target="_blank" title="7x7 California whale migration"><span style="font-weight: 400;">California’s grey whales begin their northern migration in mid-March</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, making it a peak time to witness one nature’s largest and most magnificent creatures. There are numerous spots along the </span><a href="http://gocalifornia.about.com/od/cacoast/" target="_blank" title="California coast "><span style="font-weight: 400;">California coast</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to see mothers and their calves feeding close to shore.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In </span><a href="https://www.google.com/search?q=northern+california&amp;oq=northern+california&amp;aqs=chrome..69i57j69i60j69i65j69i60l2j69i61.2493j0j9&amp;sourceid=chrome&amp;es_sm=91&amp;ie=UTF-8" target="_blank" title="Northern California"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Northern California</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, you can head to </span><a href="http://www.parks.ca.gov/?page_id=453" target="_blank" title="Salt Point Sonoma"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Salt Point</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, one of Sonoma coast’s most scenic destinations, </span><a href="http://montereybay.noaa.gov/visitor/whalewatching/welcome.html" target="_blank" title="Monterey Bay"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Monterey Bay</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, considered to be the marine equivalent of going on an African safari, or as far down south as </span><a href="http://www.sandiego.org/what-to-do/tours-sightseeing/whale-watching.aspx" target="_blank" title="San Diego Whale Sightseeing tour"><span style="font-weight: 400;">San Diego</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.  </span></p>
