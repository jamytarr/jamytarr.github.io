<h2>I've been dreaming of a drop shoulder coat for some time.</h2>
<h2>The design starting point was to create an easy coat that could be worn like a tailored cardigan. It would perfectly pair with black skinny trouser or a great pair of ripped 501s. The coat had to be built from the softest wool around and cut to a versatile length similar to a 60s car coat. I chose a 3/4 sleeve to show off your sentimental bracelets by rolling up the sleeve to give a peek at the monochromatic satin lining. Finally, the coat had to have a racing stripe down the back. This figurative wink paid homage to incredible Italian craftsmanship of Gran Turismo engineering.</h2>
<h2>Because after all, this life is a sweet ride.</h2>
