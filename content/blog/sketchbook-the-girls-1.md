<p>Color inspiration for this week's desaturated color story belongs to the green sea grass that grows on the Northern California beach dunes. This cool shade is particularly plentiful along the Point Reyes National Seashore and always gives me a sense of calm. When I close my eyes, I can almost feel the sea breeze and wet salt spray of the Pacific Ocean.</p>
