<p>Happy Friday! The weekend is upon us.</p>
<p>This week, 'The Girls' are wearing our <a href="http://jamytarr.com/collections/outerwear/products/cream-japanese-wool-moto-jacket?variant=4082640581" target="_blank" title="Moto Jacket White Cream " rel="noopener noreferrer">Moto Jacket</a>. This particular style is one of the cornerstones of the J'Amy Tarr Outerwear brand.</p>
<p>Outlets like <a href="http://jamytarr.com/blogs/press/tagged/rue-magazine" target="_blank" title="Rue Daily Magazine J'Amy Tarr " rel="noopener noreferrer"><em>Rue Magazine</em></a>, <a href="http://jamytarr.com/blogs/press/16386728-sf-chronicle-style-section-cover-light-up-the-night" target="_blank" title="SF Chronicle Style J'Amy Tarr Moto Jacket" rel="noopener noreferrer"><em>San Francisco Chronicle Style</em></a>, <a href="http://jamytarr.com/blogs/press/55941637-under-the-lights-marin-magazine-september" target="_blank" title="Marin Magazine J'Amy Tarr " rel="noopener noreferrer"><em>Marin Magazine</em></a> and <a href="http://www.7x7.com/style-design/2014s-fashion-darlings-top-mens-styles-hair-raisers-and-more" target="_blank" title="7x7 J'Amy Tarr Moto Jacket" rel="noopener noreferrer">7x7</a> have showcased and styled it in many different ways. The Moto is made from the softest lightweight Japanese wool that is woven to perfection.</p>
<p>Throw the Moto Jacket over jeans and a blouse or tee for an everyday outfit; you can even pair it with a dress or skirt for a date night look. </p>
<p>'The Girls' are styled in various Moto Jackets to show its versatility. I hold a special place in my heart for the Moto Jacket - I wear it every season! It is a timeless and flattering shape that will always feel right.</p>
