<p style="text-align: left;">Introducing a completely new design, the Capelet imbues allure, beauty and romance. The combination of the luscious fabric and the easy-to-wear design makes this new garment a show stopper, day or night. </p>
<h2>Capelet Versatility</h2>
<p>Whether you're going out on the town, to a festive holiday gathering or even to a casual date for a catch up over coffee, the Capelet is your go to garment. Wear it over a dress to give your outfit an extra boost of luxe or with dark shade jeans silk top to amp up a more casual look. </p>
<p>A slightly shorter length than our classic <a href="https://jamytarr.com/collections/outerwear/products/the-cape-in-forest-green" target="_blank" title="Forest Green British Racing Car Green Cape" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cape</a>, the Capelet hits at the small of your back.</p>
<p>But just like the <a href="https://jamytarr.com/collections/outerwear/products/the-cape-in-black?variant=919768405" target="_blank" title="Black Cape" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cape</a>, this new Capelet has slits for your arms - making it easier to hold your cocktail! </p>
<h2>Hollywood Golden Age Inspiration</h2>
<p>As we designed and sourced fabric for the capelet, the garment began to take a personality of its own. Each capelet reminded us of a glamorous old Hollywood starlet -- <a href="https://www.pinterest.com/pin/200199145916321290/" target="_blank" title="Marilyn Monroe Old Hollywood Glamour" rel="noopener noreferrer">Marilyn Monroe</a>, <a href="https://www.pinterest.com/pin/200199145916315139/" target="_blank" title="Marlene Dietrich Old Hollywood Glamour" rel="noopener noreferrer">Marlene Dietrich</a>,  <a href="https://www.pinterest.com/pin/200199145916315097/" target="_blank" title="Ava Gardner Beauty" rel="noopener noreferrer">Ava Gardner</a>, <a href="https://www.pinterest.com/pin/200199145916315147/" target="_blank" title="Eartha Kitt" rel="noopener noreferrer">Eartha Kitt</a>, <a href="https://www.pinterest.com/pin/31454897377597472/" target="_blank" title="Dorian Leigh" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dorian Leigh</a> and other beauties.  </p>
<p>Each caplet brings a star quality from this era, breathing life and luxe into your outfit.</p>
<p>The <a href="https://jamytarr.com/collections/outerwear/products/capelet-in-smoke?variant=25202198096" target="_blank" title="Smoke Capelet" rel="noopener noreferrer">Smoke Capelet</a> was inspired by hand-coloring, which is the pastel colored filter on photographs that was prominently used during Hollywood's Golden Age. This popular technique enhanced the star's features, which were originally muted by the black and white photography of the past.</p>
