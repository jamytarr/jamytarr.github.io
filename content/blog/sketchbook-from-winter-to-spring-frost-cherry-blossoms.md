<h3 style="text-align: left;">Winter is about creativity and how you choose to express your personal style through the mountains amount of layers, especially in the Bay Area - we’re looking at you chunky knits, sweaters and oversized cardigans! </h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Enter our bomber jackets, we’ve created them in various fabrics for every season and microclimate. </span></h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We create jackets from wool, leather cotton, knit blends; our fabrication runs the gamut to suit each season and occasion. </span></h3>
<p> </p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">Frost &amp; Cherry Blossom</span></h2>
<br> <meta charset="utf-8">
<p style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Late Winter Bomber Jackets Pink and White" src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/Part_4_-_Bomber_Outfits_1024x1024-74baaafb.jpg" style="float: none;"></p>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">To help with the transition from winter to spring, our new releases are the Frost and Cherry Blossom Bomber Jackets. These garments are made from a yarn dye knit blend, which helps when the temperatures drop, but they can also be worn during temperate climates in the spring.</span></h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Both Frost and Cherry Blossom Bomber Jackets have a unique texture that reminds us of cherry blossom trees. The radiant, budding flowers gives off a sense of refreshed perspective and radiant energy.</span></h3>
<p> </p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">Styling Bomber Jackets for Fall through Late Winter</span></h2>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We’ve designed bomber jackets that take care of you through the fall and late winter. Whether they’re made out of mohair wool or a featherweight blend, we’ve got you covered.</span></h3>
<meta charset="utf-8">
<h2></h2>
<h2>Fall</h2>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">When the weather is just beginning to bite and the wind is starting to have a chill, we look to our <a href="https://jamytarr.com/collections/outerwear/products/tweed-yarn-dye-bomber-jacket?variant=4366055365" target="_blank" title="Yarn Dye Tweed Bomber Jacket" rel="noopener noreferrer">Yarn Dye Bomber Jacket</a> and <a href="https://jamytarr.com/collections/outerwear/products/sapphire-leather-bomber-jacket?variant=4082678277" target="_blank" title="Sapphire Leather Bomber Jacket" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sapphire Leather Bomber Jacket</a>. </span></h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Throw on a lightweight scarf that balances out the color of the jacket and your top. A hat of any kind (we chose a wide brimmed fedora and a felt cap) to fight the cold but also giving you a sophisticated, cool edge.</span></h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">While you're taking a weekend day trip to <a href="http://mashable.com/2014/09/05/fall-activity-guide-san-francisco/" target="_blank" title="Fall Activity Guide SF" rel="noopener noreferrer">shuck oysters</a> at <a href="www.tomalesbayoystercompany.com/" target="_blank" title="Tomales Bay Oyster Company" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tomales Bay Oyster Company</a> or strolling the <a href="http://lostinsf.com/en/alameda-flea-market" target="_blank" title="Alameda Flea Market Sunday" rel="noopener noreferrer">Alameda Flea Market</a> for your next furniture purchase, these bomber jackets are perfect for just about any activity.</span></h3>
<p> </p>
<h2>Mid Winter</h2>
<meta charset="utf-8">
<p style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Mid Winter Bomber Jackets with Fur Accessories" src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/Mid_Winter_Shop_Page_1024x1024-d276825e.jpg" style="float: none;"></p>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><strong>Mid winter nights are filled with festive gatherings, friends and family. While black may be the go to choice of the season, we try to experiment with deep, rich colors - it’s a great way to stand out and show your personal style.</strong></h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><strong>No more boring black with our textured <a href="https://jamytarr.com/collections/outerwear/products/the-mohair-bomber-jacket-jet-black?variant=919793029" target="_blank" title="Mohair Wool Black Bomber Jacket" rel="noopener noreferrer">Mohair Wool Bomber Jacket</a> (it’s incredibly cozy - a fashionable blanket!) and our <a href="https://jamytarr.com/collections/outerwear/products/gunmetal-grey-jacquard-bomber?variant=4083643077" target="_blank" title="Gunmetal Grey Jacquard Bomber Jacket" rel="noopener noreferrer">Gunmetal Jacquard Bomber Jacket</a>. Jewel tone accents like the purple fur wrap or dip dyed crimson cashmere scarf with matching leather gloves are a sure-fire way to add life into an all black mid winter outfit.</strong></h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><strong>Wear these bomber jackets to <a href="http://toscacafesf.com/" target="_blank" title="Tosca Cafe San Francisco" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tosca Cafe</a> for a night out in the city while enjoying their famous <a href="http://sf.eater.com/maps/winter-cocktails" target="_blank" title="SF Eater Winter Cocktails" rel="noopener noreferrer">House "Cappuccino"</a> - a blend of Armagnac, Buffalo Trace Bourbon, organic milk and chocolate ganache from our San Francisco favorite Dandelion Chocolates.</strong></h3>
<p> </p>

<h2>Late Winter</h2>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">In the last portion of winter, we can almost taste spring. It’s so close yet the temperatures are still brisk and cold. Our solution? The Frost and Cherry Blossom Bomber Jackets.</h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">The colors look to spring but the jacket weight is still heavy enough for winter with a fine cashmere, wool or blend sweater.</h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">A beanie could be a great addition for the foggy Bay Area mornings! We occasionally throw one on while getting our morning coffee fixes from our favorite Mill Valley neighbors like <a href="https://www.equatorcoffees.com/" target="_blank" title="Equator Coffee and Tea" rel="noopener noreferrer">Equator Coffee</a> or <a href="https://www.urbanremedy.com/" target="_blank" title="Urban Remedy Neeka" rel="noopener noreferrer">Urban Remedy</a>.  </h3>
