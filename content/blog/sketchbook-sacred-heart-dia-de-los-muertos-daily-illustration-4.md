<div style="text-align: center;">This time for my Daily Practice, I challenged myself to illustrate something with a lot of details... I mean *A LOT* of details. Since I love the beautiful colors and symbols used in the DÍa de los Muertos celebrations across Mexico, I thought I'd do a google search with that in mind. After a few minutes, I came across a blogger's photo that I loved. <a href="http://thebeautifulcircus.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">The Beautiful Circus's blog</a>, I found an article she wrote to help you reproduce her outfit. If you are interested in seeing how she created it, check it our <a href="http://thebeautifulcircus.com/2018/10/15/sacred-heart-dia-de-los-muertos-costume/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.</div>
<div style="text-align: center;"></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">I absolutely love what Día de los Muertos stands for.  It brings people together annually to celebrate in life-affirming joy and pay respect to those loved ones who have passed. In the spirit, I dedicate this illustration to my mom. ❤️</div>
