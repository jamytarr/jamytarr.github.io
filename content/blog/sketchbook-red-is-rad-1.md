<h1>Red Inspiration Story</h1>
<p>The Red Wool Moto Jacket is in such a rich, vibrant red that is perfect on any and every skin tone. It brings out the best in each of us! </p>
<p>This jacket will steal the show at your next holiday party or it'll add the finishing touch to your outfit for your next family gathering.</p>
<p>More importantly, this jacket will go the distance beyond the holidays - pair it with jeans, t-shirt and flats ... you've instantly got a casual yet sophisticated outfit for any day of the week. </p>
<p>Red is passion. It's a color that stirs up emotion. Each hue of red is intense in its own way.</p>
