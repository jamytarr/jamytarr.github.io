<p>Have I told you that my brilliant friend, <a href="https://www.lisaandersonshaffer.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Lisa Anderson Shaffer</a> of <a href="https://www.zelmarose.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Zelma Rose</a> &amp; <a href="https://www.lisaandersonshaffer.com/blogs/these-three-things" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">THESE THREE THINGS,</a> and I have started a podcast called <a href="https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/biased-cuts/id1496416735" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Biased Cuts</a>? </p>
<meta charset="utf-8">
<p>We share over 30 years of experience in the fashion industry and have seen a lot. Together we dish on the warm fuzzies, big wins, huge losses, and all the bad behavior (including ours) we've encountered during our time in business. Take a listen to <a href="https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/biased-cuts/id1496416735" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Biased Cuts</a> on Apple Podcasts or anywhere you listen to podcasts for the real juice on what it takes to run an indie brand in the big, badass fashion biz. </p>
<p><a href="https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/biased-cuts/id1496416735" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/red_biased_cuts_logo_square_2048x2048-467efc63.jpg" alt="" width="2048x2048" height="2048x2048"></a></p>
<meta charset="utf-8">
<p>What started off <span>as a small concept,</span><span> has now turned into an empowering project for</span> Lisa and I to share our experiences with small to mid-size women-owned businesses. We have embraced this safe place to share what we are changing, learning and going through during this time where nothing seems like business-as-usual.</p>
