<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In the famed city by the Bay, each neighborhood has its own microclimate. Within a few blocks it may feel like a different city altogether -- hotter than normal temperatures in the </span><a href="https://www.airbnb.com/locations/san-francisco/mission-district" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mission</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, copious amounts of </span><a href="https://twitter.com/KarlTheFog?ref_src=twsrc%5Egoogle%7Ctwcamp%5Eserp%7Ctwgr%5Eauthor" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="font-weight: 400;">fog</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> in the </span><a href="https://www.airbnb.com/locations/san-francisco/richmond-district" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Richmond</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and </span><a href="https://www.airbnb.com/locations/san-francisco/inner-sunset" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Sunset</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, just to name a few.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The weather unpredictability makes getting dressed challenging -- it’s not easy to be prepared for all the elements the </span><a href="http://www.7x7.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="font-weight: 400;">7x7</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> mile city dishes out.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">San Franciscans, and even neighboring dwellers, ultimately learn to layer in mid-weight pieces.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">No matter what season, it’s always cold in the shade.</span></p>
