<p>There is something to be said about <a href="http://www.wikihow.com/Be-a-Morning-Person" target="_blank" title="Morning Person Routine">early mornings</a>, especially before it all begins. There is magic in the moment that teeters between the worlds of slumber and arousal. Excitement builds to <a href="http://www.timeanddate.com/sun/" target="_blank" title="Sunrise calculator">watch the sun slowly rise</a> over a city that is about to wake up and become alive. </p>
<p>The <a href="http://www.sfgate.com/news/article/Dense-morning-fog-sets-in-around-Bay-Area-6860554.php" target="_blank" title="Dense Morning Fog">morning fog</a> symbolizes these moments and, almost instantaneously, creates a sense of <a href="http://www.iaminfin8.com/" target="_blank" title="Chase Mirkovitz I AM INFIN8">quiet meditation</a>. </p>
<p>The fog is this silent, tranquil yet powerful force that rolls into <a href="https://www.facebook.com/places/Things-to-do-in-San-Francisco-California/114952118516947/" target="_blank" title="Things to do in San Francisco">San Francisco</a> every morning. With its billowy body it brings inspiration, awe and wonder. </p>
