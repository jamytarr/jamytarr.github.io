<p>I let my pencil zig zag along the length of the dresses and shirts. This accentuated the garment to extend well beyond the shape of the body. The color palette was inspired by the type of fire you might see on a super hero. Their shoes are reminiscent of Parisian <span>croissants</span>. Maybe I was hungry while illustrating this? I love humor in fashion.</p>
