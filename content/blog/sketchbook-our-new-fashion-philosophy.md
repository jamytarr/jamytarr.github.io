<h3><strong>September 2016 Moodboard</strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Forever inspired by quality craftsmanship and premium construction, we take a longevity-focused approach to our designs. Each silhouette, fabric choice and the overall quality control process makes our outerwear stand the test of time. </span></p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #999999;"><em>Our outerwear surpasses trends.</em></span></strong></p>
<p>This September marks the launch of our new fashion philosophy. Each month we will release a new design, giving you a refreshed approach to the fall and winter months.</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Here is a sneak preview of what’s to come for our upcoming new style launch. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img alt="September Fall Mood Board" src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/Moodboard_Image-webpage_1_1024x1024-0ece6059.jpg" style="float: none;"></p>
<p> </p>

<h2>Birds in flight</h2>
<p><span style="line-height: 1.4; font-size: 15px; font-weight: 400;"></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our first fall design debut is inspired by birds; specifically, birds from the Marin Headlands. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Birds signify freedom and perspective, which resonates with us this fall season. They’re characterized as divine creatures, bridging the gap between mundane and spiritual life - we respect that!</span></p>
<h2>
<img src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/Landing_Page_-_section_2_ba088086-f1fd-4e0e-b0eb-ef7f86c2cea0_1024x1024-3aa459c9.jpg" alt="Muir Beach San Francisco Marin Headlands">September color story</h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A polished palette of neutrals. Our color story for this fall collection is a combination of ivory, matte black and bronze. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">These colors reflect the jagged rocks along the Marin coastline, earth sediment on Mount Tamalpais and the golden hue of the setting sun on the Pacific. </span></p>
<p> </p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #666666;"><em>Stay tuned for the new style launch in mid-September. </em></span></h3>
