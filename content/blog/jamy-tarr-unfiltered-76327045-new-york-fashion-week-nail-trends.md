<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Fashion Week is here! It’s underway and in full swing with </span><a href="http://www.justjared.com/tags/2016-new-york-fashion-week-winter/" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">industry festivities</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, </span><a href="http://www.theprintuplist.com/" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">launch parties</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, </span><a href="http://nowfashion.com/categories/playing" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">runway shows</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and even </span><a href="http://pitchfork.com/news/56600-twin-shadow-performs-live-original-score-at-public-school-fashion-show/" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">concerts</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Whether you’ve only skimmed recent headlines about the latest happenings on New York Fashion Week, or you’re fully in the know, prominent design houses alike are showcasing their latest and greatest. In the midst of all of the rising trends is something that takes us back to our own </span><a href="http://dbuster.com/life/26-childhood-memories-we-miss-the-most/" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">childhood days</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> -- eccentric, slightly out of this world </span><a href="http://www.nailpro.com/gallery-nails-from-nyfw-aw-2016/" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">nail art</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">No. We’re not referencing </span><a href="http://www.mimichatter.com/easy-90s-nail-art-960996621.html" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">nail decals</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> or </span><a href="http://stylecaster.com/beauty/nail-stickers-trend/" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">acrylic nail stickers</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> as making a national debut.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Runway nails are becoming an art form.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Nails are a focal point for the audience and cameras in the front rows, so designers are now giving them the attention that they deserve. Nails can be the “punctuation mark” that brings the overall look and designers’ stories to life, as described by </span><a href="https://www.cnd.com/jan-arnold" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Jan Arnold, co-founder and style director for Creative Nail Design, Inc</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The days of </span><a href="http://opi.com/color/do-you-take-lei-away-0#fEmFqHWhRWAJxfhx.97" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">nude nails</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> are out. Statement colors and designs are in.</span></p>
