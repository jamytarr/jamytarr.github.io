<p>I have fall on my mind these days... This week, The Girls are channeling a pre-fall vibe. I decided to illustrate them in easy fall inspired shapes with rich walnut and pumpkin colors.</p>
<p>Fall development is in full swing at the J'Amy Tarr Studio and samples are trickling in. These cool Northern California temperatures are reminding me of the cozy feeling of a warm jacket on a fall evening stroll. </p>
<p>Come by the J'Amy Tarr Studio in downtown Mill Valley to see what we are working on. </p>
