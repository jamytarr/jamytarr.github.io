<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As we’re in the thick of </span><a href="http://womenshistorymonth.gov/" target="_blank" title="Women's History Month"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Women’s History Month</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, we continue to celebrate women, their creative spirit and accomplishments.</span></p>
