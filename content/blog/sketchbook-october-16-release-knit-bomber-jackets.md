<p><strong>October 2016 Moodboard</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Every month during this fall and winter season, we’re releasing a new design and accompanying moodboard. This approach is new for us; we’re forging a new path in the fashion industry with this method.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Last month we released the ‘Take Flight’ Moto Jackets in cream and black as the first debut of our new concept. For October, we were inspired by vivid, rich colors and unique textures. We searched for the perfect combination of each that caught our eye.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Golden Gate Bridge" src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/Crimson_Concrete_Moodboard_1024x1024-ec1de910.jpg" style="float: none;"></p>
<p><strong>Crimson and Concrete</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our color story for this October collection is crimson and concrete.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Crimson is a warm and positive color associated with strong, powerful energy. Historically, the color has been associated with nobility during the latter part of the Middle Ages throughout Europe. Crimson’s brazen hue drew us in and inspired us so much that we wanted to create a Bomber Jacket with it!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Because the city of San Francisco is a heavy influence for our brand, the color concrete truly stood out. The grey tinge of the city sidewalks to the rolling fog over the Golden Gate Bridge will forever remind us of the city we love so much - San Francisco.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="/shopify-assets/cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0182/5617/files/Crimson_Concrete_Moodboard_2_1024x1024-5d706024.jpg" alt="San Francisco city concrete photography"></span></p>
<p><strong>Knit Bomber Jacket</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The knit texture of this new style gives the perpetually classic Bomber Jacket a new, refreshed look. It can be a cozy and casual for the chilly Bay Area weather or can be dressed up for a night out on the town with a pair of heels or booties. </span></p>
