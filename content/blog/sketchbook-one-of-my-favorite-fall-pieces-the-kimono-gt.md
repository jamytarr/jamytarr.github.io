<div style="text-align: left;">I am a huge fan of fall style. Growing up in the SF Bay Area, I know chilly, yet often temperate, weather. This means for most of the year, I can wear a mid-weight jacket or coat over my outfit and be pretty set for the day, save for an occasional cashmere scarf or hat on those extra cold winter days.</div>
<div style="text-align: left;"></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">One of my go-to pieces is the Kimono GT coat. This is a 9 month'r meaning you can wear this from September -&gt; May and then occasionally during the "June Gloom" segment of the year as well. That's a lot. This jacket is just the right amount of warmth, style and versatility to be a favorite.</div>
<div style="text-align: left;"></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">Great styling options for the Kimono GT:</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">- Wear a single color top and pant for a polished look with a sandal.</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">- Wear a pair of deconstructed denim and a tank with mens oxfords.</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">- Wear a turtle neck with a pencil skirt &amp; an ankle boot.</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">- Wear a mini-skirt with tights and a rock n' roll tee with an ankle boot.</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">- Wear a sweater dress, tights and a tall boot with a unique heel detail.</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">- Wear a lace turtle neck dress with an ankle strap sandal.</div>
