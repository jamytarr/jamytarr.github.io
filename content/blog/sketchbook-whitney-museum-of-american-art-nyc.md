<span>Greetings from the other side.... </span><span>Of the country, that is... Hello NYC!</span>
</div>
<div style="text-align: left;"><span></span></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="text-align: center;"><span style="text-align: center;">Only recently, did I learn to include valuable downtime in my schedule to relax and reflect. Because let's get real, reflection is what it's all about. </span></span><span style="text-align: center;">Staying focused on what's important is crucial to personal and professional success. People who run their own businesses can attest, it is difficult to pull yourself away from the moment you are in, but the benefits are to be had when you take a pause. </span>
</div>
<div style="text-align: left;"><span style="text-align: center;"></span></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="text-align: center;">Last weekend I was in NYC and got a chance to hit the pause button. I was able to re-energize myself while perusing the <a href="https://whitney.org/visit?gclid=CjwKCAjw8ZHsBRA6EiwA7hw_sWOUYhzGN_x0ipv1z3MmEyMdOp7RDdbl1ahLVq8bIO9TjOCEIifqExoCaKYQAvD_BwE" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Whitney Museum of Modern Art</a>. As I walked the halls, I realized again how deeply art can affect us. </span><span>Art is something that can change your perspective. The act of making it, viewing it or engaging in it can elevate your mood, make you think in a new way and create joy. I believe it </span><span>strengthens focus and strategic thinking, enhances creativity and is a valuable tool to express yourself. </span>
</div>
