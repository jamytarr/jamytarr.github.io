<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Originally emanating from the world of </span><a href="http://www.harley-davidson.com/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Harley Davidsons</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, the prominent moto jackets of today are now </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">the ultimate wardrobe perk-me-ups — perfect to toughen up charming and girlish garments, ideal to complement a look that’s equally edgy.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Moto jackets are multifaceted and timeless. These pieces pave the way for a more polished, coordinated outfit in minutes.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Invest in a quality jacket that will stand the test of time. While trends throughout each season’s </span><a href="http://mbfashionweek.com/" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Fashion Week</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> change, the moto jacket continues to be a perennial favorite.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Here are some tips on how to select the perfect moto jacket:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<b>Fabric is everything</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Experiment with different types of fabric or leather to create distinct looks. Have a more edgy, urban allure? Opt for a leather moto jacket. Want clean lines and classic style? Try for a wool blend, similar to </span><a href="http://jamytarr.com/collections/all" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">J’Amy Tarr’s Japanese wool</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<b>Pay attention to construction</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Rely on the capital rule of quality versus quantity when purchasing timeless garments. Garment seams should be feminine and flatter the natural flow of the body. Zippers, like stitch lines, should always lay flat and never appear wavy.</span>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<b>Accent with hardware</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Moto jackets are typically paired with asymmetrical zippers, studs, buckles or other bells and whistles. Ensure that each hardware piece doesn’t overwhelm, but, instead, flatters the body and final outfit choice. A more conservatively designed moto jacket may include fewer zippers while those appearing on the runway may showcase embellishments for an editorial effect.</span>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<b>Play with proportions</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Explore various lengths and volumes of moto jackets to find the perfect match for individual body types and styles. Cropped (like this one from </span><a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/browse/leather-jackets/iro" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">IRO</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">), hip-length (try this one from </span><a href="http://www.rebeccataylor.com/jackets-coats-and-outerwear/metallic-leather-moto-jacket/invt/rt615415j605?source=239_75&amp;cm_mmc=google-_-shopping:%20product%20listing%20ads-_-sweaters%20&amp;%20cardigans-_-N&amp;gclid=COmy_7Gs3MoCFc2CfgodBKUBvg" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rebecca Taylor</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">) or oversized (take a look at </span><a href="http://www.acnestudios.com/shop/women/coats-jackets/more-black.html" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Acne Studios</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">’ style) are ever popular.</span>
</li>
</ul>
