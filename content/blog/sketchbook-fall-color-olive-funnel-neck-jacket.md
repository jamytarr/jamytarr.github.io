<h3 style="text-align: left;">The Olive Funnel Neck is the newest iteration of our favorite fall jacket. This style was designed with chilly mornings, crisp afternoons and breezy evenings in mind. Northern Californian's know that having thermo-versatility is key to dressing during these transitional months. </h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">Enter the Funnel Neck Jacket. This jacket has a figure flattering shape that fits like a glove. The versatility comes from the diagonal center front zipper. Depending upon how far up you zip this jacket, you get a multitude of functionality.</h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">-Keep this jacket unzipped for a longer Motorcycle jacket shape with exaggerated lapels. </h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">-Zip it up to just above your waist to emphasize your hourglass shape. The diagonal design seaming adds to the shaping. To make the lapel smaller, just adjust by tucking in to the desired width.</h3>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">-For ultimate warmth, zip up jacket completely creating the "funnel" around your neck. Make sure to fold over each lapel by overlapping them when you zip it all the way up. This double layer of ultra soft brushed Japanese Wool at your neck allows you to leave your scarf at home!</h3>
