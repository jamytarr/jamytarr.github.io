<p>Designers are perennially inspired by the fitted clean lines of a shrunken blazer or crisp white button down. What better time for an ode to the familiar school uniform. </p>

<p><a href="http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-england-29047752" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">The origins of the school uniform</a> are said to have begun in charity schools in England in the mid 1500s.</p>
