<p>Happy Friday! This version of The Girls is inspired by the foggy Bay Area weekend. The city's colors is muted down to a tonal palette and neutral grey. </p>
<p>These looks are illustrated with a variety of grey tones from the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zxA65qY3bko" target="_blank" title="Copic Marker tutorial" rel="noopener noreferrer">Copic Sketch Marker collection</a>, which is one of my favorite art supply tools to use! It makes illustrating these Girls so much better. This week, even if its summer time, The Girls are keeping cool in the spirit of the Bay Area's notorious <a href="https://twitter.com/KarlTheFog" target="_blank" title="Karl the Fog" rel="noopener noreferrer">Karl the Fog</a>. </p>
