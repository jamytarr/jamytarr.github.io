<h3>The first time I met Kendall Chase I knew she had star power.</h3>
<h3>On <a href="https://www.instagram.com/chasingkendall/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Instagram as @chasingkendall</a>, she has created a visual wonderland to showcase her fashion point of view - and it's a good one. She's naturally drawn to combining clothing and accessories in unique and unexpected ways. Sometimes fashion can be predictable, so it is refreshing to see someone rethink it.</h3>
<h3>When Kendall styled our <a href="https://jamytarr.com/collections/outerwear/products/tux-cape-in-dark-floral" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dark Floral Tux Cape</a>, she chose to pair it with a menswear inspired crisp button down shirt. Replete with details including french cuffs and embroidery monograms, she took our Tux Cape to new heights. </h3>
<h3>See her post <a href="http://chasingkendall.com/celebrate-holidays-sf/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here </a>about how she created a gorgeous holiday look that can wear year round.</h3>
