<p> We want you to be completely happy with your order. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we will gladly accept your return or exchange of unworn, unwashed and unaltered merchandise within 15 days of receipt of goods. <p>Once goods are received in original condition, a full refund will be made. </p>

Returns should be shipped back in original packaging. Purchaser is responsible for return shipping costs. </p>
